About Beeline

Beeline Learn (Pty) Ltd builds Beeline — a workforce performance and intelligence platform for frontline and distributed teams. Organisations assign outcomes like KPIs, SOPs, and role expectations, and Beeline delivers targeted learning via web, mobile, and WhatsApp. The system captures real-world evidence and performance signals, learns what works, and continuously improves workforce capability. More than just training software — Beeline is built for operational teams where performance, not course completion, matters.

Our purpose is to maximise realised human potential by democratising effective learning.

Our platform runs at beeline.life while our primary company domain is beelinelearn.com.
